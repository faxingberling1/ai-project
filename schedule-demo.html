Demo FAQs

How long does the demo typically last?
Our standard demos last approximately 45 minutes, including time for Q&A. For more complex use cases or technical deep dives, we can schedule extended sessions up to 90 minutes.
Who should attend the demo?
We recommend including key decision-makers from both technical and business teams. This typically includes product managers, technical leads, and executives who will be involved in the implementation decision.
What preparation is needed before the demo?
No specific preparation is required. However, if you have particular use cases, challenges, or questions you'd like us to address, sharing them in advance helps us tailor the demo to your needs.
Can I request specific features to be shown?
Absolutely! We encourage you to specify any particular features, integrations, or use cases you're most interested in. This allows us to focus the demo on what matters most to your business.
