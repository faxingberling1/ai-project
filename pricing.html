Frequently Asked Questions

Can I change plans anytime?
Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll be charged the prorated difference for the remainder of your billing cycle. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
Is there a free trial available?
Yes, we offer a 14-day free trial for all our plans. You'll have full access to all features during the trial period. No credit card is required to start your trial.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for annual plans. All payments are processed securely through our payment partners.
Do you offer discounts for non-profits?
Yes, we offer a 25% discount for registered non-profit organizations. Please contact our sales team with proof of your non-profit status to apply for this discount.
How does the API call limit work?
API calls are counted whenever you make a request to our AI models. This includes text generation, image processing, data analysis, and any other interactions with our AI services. Unused API calls do not roll over to the next month.
Can I cancel my subscription anytime?
Absolutely. You can cancel your subscription at any time from your account settings. If you cancel during a billing period, you'll retain access to all features until the end of that period.
