Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our pricing and services.

Can I mix and match services from different tiers?

Yes! Our platform is designed to be flexible. You can combine services from different tiers to create a custom solution that fits your specific needs. Contact our sales team for assistance with custom configurations.

How do AI requests work across different services?

AI requests are counted across all services. Each interaction (chat message, data analysis, workflow execution, etc.) counts as one request. Your monthly limit is shared across all AI services you use.

Do you offer custom enterprise solutions?

Absolutely! Our Enterprise plan is fully customizable to meet your specific requirements. We can create tailored solutions with custom pricing, dedicated infrastructure, and specialized features. Contact our sales team to discuss your needs.

Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan later?

Yes, you can change your plan at any time. When upgrading, you'll get immediate access to the new features. When downgrading, the changes will take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.

What happens if I exceed my monthly AI request limit?

If you exceed your monthly limit, you can purchase additional request packs or upgrade your plan. We'll notify you when you're approaching your limit to avoid any service interruptions.
